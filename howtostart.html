How to start?

Home About How to start?

  • Step 1

    For the beginning you need to be diagnosed. You don’t have diagnosis yet ? How to get it ? To learn more about it click here.


  • Step 2

    Find relevant course/education for yourself or your relative. You can get a course refunded by the kommune - to get more information about it contact one of our counselors via email centerrefund@autisme.dk. Or maybe you are looking for a support group ? Or work possibilities ? You can find all the information over here.


  • Step 3

    Come visit our center and have a trial day before making a decision. You will have the possibility to see how our professional work, what we are doing during the day, who is join our groups etc.


  • Step 4

    If you will be interested after trial days you should provide relevant documents(such as diagnosis or any other medical documents, support from the kommune) and sign up the contract.


  • Step 5

    You are ready to be a part of Center for Autisme.

Newsletter

When you sign up for our newsletter, you can expect to receive newsletters with content from all our various departments here at the Center for Autism. You will always have the opportunity to unsubscribe if it does not have your interest.
