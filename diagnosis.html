Information


The Center for Autism offers different types of investigation of children and adults, where a diagnosis is suspected or already made within the autistic spectrum or other neurological development disorder ( intellectual disability, ADHD or specific language disorders).


    Location and duration:

    After a possible pre-interview, the actual investigation will typically be carried out on the same day. It usually lasts 6-7 hours and usually takes place at the Center for Autism. If it is considered relevant, an observation is also included in the person's daily environment by agreement.


    Price:

    Kr. 36,700 (full diagnostic inquiry)
    Kr. 29,500 (full diagnostic examination, toddlers 0-4 years)
    The course and price of the report are agreed individually, after which the final agreement is submitted for signature. Supplements may occur.


Available examination


  • Neuropsychological examination

    The overall purpose of a neuropsychological study is to elucidate the citizen's level of functioning in relation to a neuropsychological problem and thus be able to contribute to promoting the citizen's development opportunities and seeing these in relation to his or her everyday life and circumstances.


  • Examination of language and communication


      Audience:

      Children (0-6 years) with a diagnosis within the autism spectrum as well as young adults with autism spectrum disorder (ASF) with delayed development.


      Purpose:

      Experience shows that in everyday life it can be difficult to get a real picture of the person's linguistic level of understanding, whether they have a sparse spoken language or an immediately well-developed spoken language, as this in itself does not say enough about what the person actually understands.


      It is crucially important that we use the right means of communication adapted to the individual with autism when we organize the educational work. The most appropriate type of communication and form can vary greatly from person to person, as it depends on the person's mental level as well as the specific level of both language comprehension and spoken language. as the specific level of both language comprehension and spoken language.


      Content and course

      The Center for Autism offers the following essential elements:
      - What type of visual information (symbol system) is meaningful?
      - Language comprehension level, and therefore, what linguistic level should be used for verbal inquiries?
      The report includes linguistic / communicative tests, interviews and questions for institution / parents.


      Subsequently, a written report is prepared that contains an assessment of the person's linguistic and communicative level of development, as well as suggestions for appropriate strategies in the linguistic and communicative field.


      The report concludes with a meeting of approx. 1½ hour duration, where both institution and possibly. parents participate. The focus of the meeting will be a feedback on the testing and a discussion of the specific instructions.


      Price:

      The course and price of the report are agreed individually, after which the final agreement is submitted for signature.


  • Psychological functional examination


      Audience:

      The group of adolescents / adults at any stage of development who have previously been diagnosed with autism spectrum or have another neurological development disorder (eg, intellectual disability, specific language disorder or ADHD)


      Purpose:

      There may be different purposes for a psychological function study.


      Content and participants:

      Prior to the investigation , a preliminary interview can be entered into, where the course of the investigation is agreed upon and reviewed. In the review will usually be included testing and interviewing the person depending t on the purpose and what there is of previous studies. In some cases, educational staff, parents or other networks are involved for an interview or questionnaire response.


      Location and duration:

      The investigation typically takes place at the Center for Autism and usually proceeds on the same day.
