Special education

Home Job and Education Special education

Purpose

The team offers help in all citizens' environments and supports social relationships, such as family challenges. Mentor support is one of the most effective tools for maintaining jobs and education for people with autism spectrum disorders and similar challenges. For example, in order to develop tools and strategies to commit socially at work, during the education with the family / girlfriend, etc.


The citizen is offered special educational support and guidance

  • To make everyday life work with food, washing, economy etc.
  • In connection with educational or work issues
  • In relation to leisure life, where there are guides to exploring opportunities, finding and possibly. test specific, relevant offers with support
  • By challenges in social contexts and in relationships.


Target group

Young people and adults with an autism spectrum diagnosis.


We offer:

  • Introduction to challenges of autism and neighboring issues
  • Educational strategies and tools
  • Sparring and supervision for staff at institutions, housing communities, mentors and others with similar work functions


Contact us, and we can talk about the possibilities of putting together a course. As a starting point, the price will be 610 kr - per. support hours.
