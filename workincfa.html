Work in CfA

Home Vacanies Work in CfA

  • We are a large house with 43 employees and many different professional groups, and a house with many users in everyday life.

  • We offer a dynamic workplace with an easy and informal tone and are in a constantly exciting development.

  • We prioritize well-being at work and many of our employees have many years of seniority.

  • We draw on psychiatric consultancy and supervision from and collaborate with other professionals.


Psychologist applied for Center for Autism, Herlev

  • Working hours per week: 37 hours


We are looking for a psychologist for our center, preferably with specialist training, as well as with knowledge and experience in autism and other neurodevelopmental disorders in children, adolescents and adults. Experience with approach and treatment of comorbid conditions will also be an advantage. Our work within investigations and advice is a core area that one is expected to be able to include, for example. a number of VISO tasks are performed on complex cases in the municipalities. In addition, we would like our new psychologist to take on teaching assignments. The work involves a high degree of autonomy and responsibility for one’s own cases, but also requires that one can enter into close cooperation with colleagues and other professionals.

You will be part of the CfA clinical team, which today consists of 11 people, mainly psychologists who together cover a wide field of the centre’s main activities including:

  • Examination of autism symptoms, comorbid conditions, and neuropsychological functions

  • Counseling and supervision for specialist groups in the normal and special field

  • Treatment tasks eg in relation to anxiety problems, OCD mm

  • Supportive conversations, and psycho-induction

  • Advisory and supportive conversations with parents and other relatives

  • Teaching and course activities



Start-up in August 2019 or by further agreement. Salary according to current agreement. For further information, please contact: Head of department: srs@centerforautisme.dk or by phone: 26 25 28 56. Application deadline: June 3, 2019.


The application is emailed to department head Susanne Henri Rasmussen at info@centerforautisme.dk
