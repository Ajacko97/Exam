How to get diagnosis?

Home About How to get diagnosis?

Do you suspect that you or your relevant have an autism spectrum ?

  • If yes, first that you have to do is sign up for a diagnosis. We offer different types of examination, to see more click here.

  • If you have trouble deciding which one is relevant for you, don’t hesitate to contact us via e-mail: diagnosis@center.dk or phone number +45 71 23 24 25.

  • After talking with a specialist he will help you choose the best one.


  • If you will decide with examination you should take, you are going to be asked about the date that fits you the best. When arriving to a center you have to go to the reception and there you will get further information. Be prepared that it will take approximately 7-9 hours.


Procedure

Adults and children will be offered an interview where the investigation and content of the investigation will be reviewed and planned. The actual study will typically include a diagnostic interview with relevant relatives or other caregivers and a diagnostic test and / or interview with the person himself. It is our experience that a person can best be assessed and helped if all important persons around him / her are involved.


Additional information is obtained by filling in various forms. In cases where commonly used psychological and speech pedagogical tests have not already been performed, we can do these in connection with the investigation.

Newsletter

When you sign up for our newsletter, you can expect to receive newsletters with content from all our various departments here at the Center for Autism. You will always have the opportunity to unsubscribe if it does not have your interest.
