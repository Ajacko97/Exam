Parking:


There is a limited number of parking spaces at the Center for Autism, so we recommend that you arrive in good time, so possibly. extra walking distance is included. Parking is possible in the side streets around the Center for Autism.

NOTE: Parking at Herlev Hovedgade in the discount is prohibited.
